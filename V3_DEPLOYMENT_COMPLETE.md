## 🎓 KEY METRICS EXPLAINED

### Performance Index (0-100)
**What it means:** Overall driver performance score
- 95-100: Legendary
- 85-94: Professional
- 75-84: Expert
- 65-74: Intermediate
- <65: Developing

### Anomaly Rate (%)
**What it means:** % of unusual events detected
- <2%: Excellent
- 2-5%: Good
- 5-10%: Fair
- >10%: Needs improvement

### Driver Style Scores
**What they mean:** Profile in 4 dimensions
- Aggressive: Throttle and steering intensity
- Smooth: Input progressiveness
- Consistent: Lap-to-lap repeatability
- Defensive: Conservative driving approach

### Consistency (0-100)
**What it means:** How repeatable your performance is
- >80: Highly consistent
- 60-80: Good consistency
- 40-60: Moderate variance
- <40: High variance
